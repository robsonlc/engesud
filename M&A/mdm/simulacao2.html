Acquisition Proposal: Mademax

Proposed Payment Structure

  • 60 monthly installments of US$ 750,000, providing a manageable and predictable payment structure.
  • Total value of US$ 45,000,000 (subject to adjustments), reflecting the fair value and potential of Mademax assets.
  • Monthly adjustment according to an agreed-upon index, ensuring fairness and value adjustment over time.

Transition Model

  • The acquiring company gradually takes over the operation of Mademax, allowing for a smooth and efficient integration.
  • The operational results obtained during the transition period contribute to the payment of installments, aligning the interests of both parties.
  • Progressive transfer of asset ownership, ensuring a safe and transparent transition.
