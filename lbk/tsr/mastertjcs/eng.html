Investment Information

This unique investment opportunity presents:

  • Total area of 200 hectares.
  • Enhanced GSV due to zoning that allows vertical buildings throughout the entire area.
  • Direct negotiation with three groups of owners, enabling partnership or acquisition.
  • Essential infrastructure for viability (responsibility of the investor/developer):
    • Maritime pier to divert waste from the Tijucas River.
    • Beach mud drainage.
    • Landfill of approximately 2 meters on the terrain plateau.
  • Estimated infrastructure cost: R$ 30 million (approximately US$ 4,838,709.68 *).

*Based on an exchange rate of R$ 6.20 per US$ (subject to change).

Smart City Tijucas

Wealth Corridor

Tijucas is strategically located in the heart of the Wealth Corridor, a region that concentrates 37% of the country's GDP and is experiencing accelerated growth.

Tijucas: A New Development Hub

With a privileged location on the coast of Santa Catarina, Tijucas attracts new investments and offers an excellent quality of life.

Economic and Population Growth

Tijucas is experiencing significant population and economic growth, driven by the arrival of new businesses and the migration of people seeking a better life.

Challenges of the Future and Urban Solutions

The world faces challenges such as the scarcity of natural resources, the need for renewable energy sources, and the importance of sustainability. Smart Cities offer solutions to these challenges.

Sustainable Mobility

Prioritizing public transportation, bike paths, and green spaces, Tijucas encourages sustainable mobility, reducing environmental impact and promoting the health of the population.

Smart Resource Management

Smart management of energy, water, and waste is fundamental to sustainable development. Tijucas invests in innovative technologies and practices to ensure the efficient use of natural resources.

A New Urban Concept

Smart City Tijucas is designed based on a new urban concept, integrating different systems harmoniously and promoting quality of life.

Centrality and Vitality

Tijucas values centrality, creating vibrant and inviting public spaces that encourage social interaction and cultural life.

Compact Urban Centers

The creation of compact and mixed-use urban centers reduces the need for travel, promotes vitality, and contributes to urban sustainability.

Successful Strategies

Tijucas implements strategies that have proven effective in other Smart Cities, such as prioritizing pedestrians, creating green spaces, and promoting public safety.

Proposal Structure

The Smart City Tijucas proposal is structured in different systems that complement each other, ensuring the integrated development of the city.

Structural Guidelines

The structural guidelines guide the development of Smart City Tijucas, prioritizing sustainability, mobility, quality of life, and the integration of different systems.

Volumetric Essay

The volumetric essay demonstrates the feasibility of the proposal and the integration of different land uses, ensuring the creation of a harmonious and functional urban environment.

Intelligent Road System

The Tijucas road system is designed to ensure traffic flow, prioritize pedestrians and cyclists, and integrate different modes of transportation.

The Waterfront and Linear Park

The waterfront and linear park are key elements of Smart City Tijucas, providing spaces for leisure, recreation, and contact with nature, as well as contributing to the improvement of the urban microclimate.

Green Areas and Public Facilities

Tijucas invests in high-quality green areas and public facilities, providing spaces for leisure, recreation, and social interaction, as well as contributing to the health and well-being of the population.

Sectors and Land Uses

Smart City Tijucas is divided into sectors with different land uses, ensuring harmony between residential, commercial, and service areas, as well as preserving green and recreational areas.

Reference of Success

Tijucas draws inspiration from successful examples of other Smart Cities around the world, adapting best practices to its local needs and characteristics.

Invest in Tijucas

Tijucas offers a favorable environment for investments, with a growing economy, a privileged location, and a new urban concept that prioritizes quality of life and sustainability.
